About Us

In India, the last drawback faced by the farmers is that the selection of correct yield for farming. Several factors influence the yield of crops like rain, temperature, soil, etc. Crop prediction helps farmers in choosing the right crop for plantation to maximize their earning.

Prediction of the crop is completed by considering parameters just like the quantity of rain, least and most temperature, soil type, humidity, and soil, pH, scale price. the knowledge is collected from the agricultural website of geographical area. the knowledge is split into 9 agricultural zones.

The associate interface has been designed through that farmers will enter the specified information to predict the crop. Classification of soil is required so that farmers can recognize the sort of soil and will plow the crops counting on the sort of soil.
